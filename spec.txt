Our app uses googleMaps and openWeather so when a user clicks on a location,
it displays the current weather for that location.

What problem are you solving?
    People can use this for knowing the weather of the location that they're
    traveling to.
Who are the stakeholders? (e.g. business leads, marketing, engineering)
    Rich is the money man and business lead. Theresa markets our fabulous project
    to potential students! "Wow look what we teach!". Engineering is the tremendous
    trio of Kyle, Kyle, and Jonathan.
User stories
    User wants to know the weather of a location and clicks on that location.
    User can search for a zip code and the map moves to that location, and displays that current weather
    User can save locations for future use.
    User sees current location when app loads.
